In this video, we can hear Okamoto-san explanation on why he wants to create a game for the Roblox platform.

He says that in Roblox, creators can create a game very quickly and also release it so that many people could try it out. He thinks that he only needs one or two people to accomplish this task. Furthermore, according to Okamoto-san, Roblox's industry is in the trillions of yen.

Next, Okamoto-san explains "Roblox Builders," who are architects that put blocks in a specific area in order to build objects like houses and buildings.

In fact, he himself has tried to create something, and reveals that Ms. Tanu is also capable of creating a game very easily. At this point, he reiterates that Ms. Tanu can already drive in Malaysia, something that helps Okamoto-san very much. Tanu-san reasons that it's because the environment as well as the surrounding areas are easy for her to recognize.

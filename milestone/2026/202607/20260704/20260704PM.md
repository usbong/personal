> Stories from Game Producer Yoshiki Okamoto: Video game creators can hardly achieve FIRE (Financial Independence, Retire Early)

> Writer: [Michael B. Syson](https://www.linkedin.com/in/michaelsyson/)

> Publication Date: 2026-07-05

Dear friends,

Today I'd like to share with you Mr. Yoshiki Okamoto's lecture about a very common concern in that video game creators can hardly achieve FIRE (Financial Independence, Retire Early).

## --

First, Okamoto-san reveals that, in reality, people who do managerial work could achieve FIRE. However, not all video game creators do managerial work in addition to creating video games. He explains that while a creator has launched a bestselling title and has made a name for himself, it doesn't mean that he would get a lot of money from the studio.

Next, Okamoto-san gives examples like Mr. Shigeru Miyamoto who does work as a corporate executive while producing titles for Nintendo, such that he's achieved a lot of success, of course, in financial terms as well. 

Finally, Okamoto-san believes that the system in Japan has to be improved just like what studios outside Japan do, that is, grant shares to employees, so that they could make more money alongside the success of their respective studios.

> Stories from Game Producer Yoshiki Okamoto: The Rolex watch is a luxury item that is more than just about telling the time

Dear friends,

Today let me share with you Mr. Yoshiki Okamoto's lecture two days ago about the Rolex watch brand, which according to his explanation, signifies a luxury item that is more than just about telling the time.

Okamoto-san shares with us that the Rolex watch symbolizes success, so people, who want to show that they've achieved success in life, buy this very high-class watch. 

Okamoto-san also explains that if we compare it with a watch from the 100 YEN SHOP, we'd find that the former costs tens of thousands of yen more than the latter. Further, Okamoto-san reveals that Rolex watches are sold only for a limited period of time in which they aren't released anymore afterward; thus, the prices of these old watches go up some more. Moreover, in fact, there's a "marathon" to collect these watches.

With regard to this point, Okamoto-san admits that almost all the Rolex watches that he buys are second-hand, because he doesn't have a lot of extra money to always buy news ones. However, he collects what he buys, because he knows that their values go up much later.

Finally, Okamoto-san concludes that given that nowadays more people have the money to buy these Rolex watches, he foresees that their values would also continue to go up even more in the near future. For this reason, the cycle of buying and collecting more of these watches by rich people will simply continue on from hereon.

> Stories from Game Producer Yoshiki Okamoto: The new G-SHOCK watch that is launching on the 17th of this month to commemorate Pokémon's 30th anniversary

> Publication Date: 2026-07-14

Dear friends,

Today let me share with you Mr. Yoshiki Okamoto's lecture about the new G-SHOCK watch that is launching on the 17th of this month to commemorate Pokémon's 30th anniversary.

Okamoto-san is very excited to hear about this collaboration, sharing that he really likes the design, including the box of the watch. In addition, the watch has the general features like the stopwatch with buttons in green and blue, analog watch hands as well as digital display, LED and many more. It's also indicated that it will cost 33,000 yen, a very low price according to Okamoto-san.

While Okamoto-san still hasn't reserved one, he thinks that it'll be a good gift, especially because it's the 30th anniversary of the beloved franchise, and if he would have to wait for the 40th anniversary, he'll already be 75 years old. Okamoto-san is therefore really fascinated by this watch, which will also be a good addition to his collection.

Finally, Okamoto-san encourages us to buy one if we're interested, saying that he wants to speak more about his love for Pokémon in another video.

> Stories from Game Producer Yoshiki Okamoto: Steam has surpassed 1.79 trillion yen in sales for the first half of the year, an achievement of about five times its sales 10 years ago

> Writer: [Michael B. Syson](https://www.linkedin.com/in/michaelsyson/)

> Publication Date: 2026-07-25

## --

Dear friends,

Today let me share with you Mr. Yoshiki Okamoto's lecture about the Steam platform, which has surpassed 1.79 million yen in sales for the first half of the year, meaning that it's achieved about five times its sales a decade ago.

> Steamの今年上半期の売上が約1兆7900億円「10年前の約5倍」 売上が爆上がり中とその理由は

## --

First, Okamoto-san explains that if we compare the available machines including video game consoles to play a game like Resident Evil Requiem in terms of graphics, he thinks that the "Gaming PC" is the winner.

In addition, while there are always misinformation, Okamoto-san believes that Steam strives to tell us the correct information, such that developers of indie games like "Meccha Camereon" could make money. 

Okamoto-san also points out that new as well as old games are sold on Steam, so players who want to play prior games of a franchise have the opportunity to play them. Further, Okamoto-san informs us that 21% of the games that are released on Steam are new, although this figure continues to go down every year.

Finally, Okamoto-san encourages us to play games on Steam, and while it's said that life isn't only about playing, if there's a desire on our part to play or collect games one day, this platform is what he'd recommend. Also, although Gaming PCs now currently cost 300,000 to 500,000 yen, in order to play old games, we don't really need to spend so much.

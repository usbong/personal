Okamoto-san recounts that it's because his bank in Malaysia had told him that he's won in the lottery. From Okamoto-san's recollection, he hasn't bought any lottery ticket at all, though he's grateful for getting a free vacation in a high-class hotel in the "Langkawi" island. Okamoto-san also reveals that it's the second time that he's won a raffle draw in Malaysia, such that he thinks that there are surely forces outside his control that are working behind the scenes to help him.

Next, Okamoto-san shows us what can be seen there including food such as whiskies with lemons and biscuits, saying that he's staying in a hotel for three days and two nights, in which every night costs 300,000 yen. Furthermore, he tells us that from this island, visitors can already go to Thailand by kayak due to its proximity.

Finally, Okamoto-san admits that he's visited this same hotel before when he and his colleagues at work had stayed there for six days and five nights. Right now, he continues to take walks every morning as well as relax in the pool, things that Okamoto-san likes very much. He also adds that this time around, he'd like to join the guided tours within the island and share with us more photos and videos.
